直接打开play搜索安装即可。

碰到的问题：**安卓ChatGPT Something went wrong. Please make sure your device has the latest version of Google Play**

参考：[安卓ChatGPT Something went wrong. Please make sure your device has the latest version of Google Play - 知乎 (zhihu.com)](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/669053528)

解决：到[apkmirror](https://www.apkmirror.com/apk/google-inc/google-play-store/google-play-store-39-2-37-release/google-play-store-39-2-37-21-0-pr-597374538-3-android-apk-download/)下载google play最新版本。选择apk包，否则新问题是使用apk installer安装bundler包之后打开google play网络连接不上。
